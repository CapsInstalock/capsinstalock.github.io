Welcome to CapsInstaLock's Prototype Page!

Have fun and take a look around. Keep in mind that all of these games are prototypes with only a few hours/days of development, so expect things to not be very polished, regardless, we hope you enjoy them!

All games (except for Rara) require the Unity Web Player, you can download it here .

SHADOW GRASP

You have awoken in what appears to be an abandoned school. What's more, you appear to have become a ghost! However, you can't seem to pass through objects yet. Can you escape the school before your light runs out? Careful, there's a shadowy hand lurking in the dark ready to grab any unfortunate soul. Developed by Chips in Another Bag for Manila Game Jam @ Benilde 2015

MIKI VS. THE RED BUTTON

Can you pass the obstacle course, collect the screws, and press the red button at the end? Developed by Chips in a Bag for Unity Game Jam 2014

THE UNLIKELY ADVENTURES OF RARA (UPDATED)

Rara needs to escape from her cave to meet [Yellow], her knight [best friend]. Can she avoid the obstacles, enemies and bugs (we're not talking about insects) that stand in her way? Developed by Water Banana.

WOOBLES

Oh no! Woobles the Jellyfish has been separated from his group during the big migration. Help him make it to his new home.

MOODSWING

A riddle, a room color, and many random objects. Can you find what the riddle is describing before time runs out? You only have 5 minutes. Developed by CIL (Team Orts) for Manila Global Game Jam @ Benilde

KINGDUMB

That dumb king has fallen in his own dangerous dungeon, it is up to the "Dungeon Master" and the king's fairy to guide him out.
